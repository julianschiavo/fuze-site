Community Guidelines

fuze empowers everyone to live in the moment, watch life happen, and have fun. We are excited to bring fuze to the world and see what you create.

We've created a set of guidelines that keep everyone safe while encouraging our principles. We are collectively responsible to keep fuze fun and safe, and these guidelines apply to everyone.

We've tried our best to outline the content and behaviour expected on fuze, but we can't anticipate everything, so we reserve the right to use our discretion to inform how we apply these guidelines and moderate fuze.

Bullying & Harassment

Do not bully, harass, threaten, or intimidate users, or post or share content that would make someone else feel bad. Do not target, degrade, or shame people for any reason.

Cruelty & Violence

Do not post or share content containing graphic violence or gore, encouraging violence or attacks, or promoting hateful or extremist ideologies. Terrorist organizations and hate groups are prohibited from using fuze. Do not promote, encourage, or depict self injury or self harm.

Illegal Content

Do not use the platform for illegal activites, related to, but not limited to, transactions involving illegal products or substances, contraband, counterfeit goods, or illegal weapons. Do not post content that contravenes your local laws or the laws of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China, in which fuze is based, such as content that can pose a threat to national security.

Safety

Do not participate in dangerous stunts or challenges, or encourage others to do so.

Politics

We support the right to freedom of expression and freedom of speech, while abiding by the local laws where we and you are based. To abide with local laws of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region ("HKSAR") in the People's Republic of China ("PRC"), and laws of the PRC itself, content that is closely related to, based around, references, could harm, or endangers, the politics of the HKSAR and/or PRC, or national security of the HKSAR and/or PRC, is prohibited and we may be required to report this content to relevant authorities.

Sexually Explicit Content

Do not post or share content that contains sexual acts, body parts, or sexually suggestive content. Child sexual exploitation is illegal, and will be reported to authorities. Never post or share explicit imagery depicting or containing minors.

Spam, Impersonation, and Deception

Do not impersonate others, or pretend to be someone else. Do not spam or deceive, including through manipulative content. Do not spread false information that has the potential to cause harm or is damaging to others or the rule of law.

Reports

While we try our best, it would not be possible for us to check every single piece of content uploaded to fuze. If you see something that you think may go against these guidelines, please report it inside the app by tapping the ellipsis button and then tapping "report".

Violations

Content that we deem to violate these guidelines will be removed, and may terminate, disable, or temporarily suspend your account. In some cases, violations will be reported to law enforcement. Users that are banned from fuze will be unable to use fuze in the future.
